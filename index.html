Object of Make-Shift - Get your three colored stones in one vertical, horizontal, or diagonal row.

Playing Make-Shift - On each turn, place an unplayed stone, move one of your stones to an empty push-stone, or push a black push-stone into the empty space. A pushed colored stone rides along with its black push-stone.

The eight black push-stones start around an empty center.

Push one neighboring black stone into the empty space.

If the empty space is on an edge, two stones in that line may slide together.

The opponent may not immediately undo the exact push just made.
